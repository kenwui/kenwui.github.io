Lakers vs Bulls


Le Sserafim Lakers vs Newjeans Bulls is an independent project which involves combining a past interest in kpop and a new interest in basketball. The process of creating this project was to pursue an area of digital media which Ken had always taken an interest in, illustration. The project was done using Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator, taking up to 2 weeks to complete. Throughout the project, Ken had the opportunity to develop and refine his creative practice in illustration, after which, upon completion, was shared to the reddit forum for the kpop groups, Le Sserafim and Newjeans.
