About



Ken is a digital media practitioner from the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology, located in Naam/Melbourne.

Ken specializes in the field of Digital Media which includes, but not limited to, video, sound and web design; he enjoys working with peers to ideate, innovate and create various forms of media work.

Ken holds a Diploma in Interactive & Digital Media as well as a Bachelor of Design (Digital Media) degree.

Over the course of 6 years, Ken has amassed plenty of technical skills in the use of various digital media related software such as the Adobe suite, blender, Figma and more.
