Drowning Screaming Forest Souls


Drowning Scremaing Forest Souls in an auditory project which involves heavy research into multisensory experiences culminating in the creation of a noise track which conveys the feeling of brutality. The mix is paired with a tactile audio system called the SubPac. The SubPac's uses has ranged from gaming to providing an auditory platform which is able to assist users who are audibly challenged.
