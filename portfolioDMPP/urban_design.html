Sister Cities


Sister Cities is a collaborative effort of 5 members to design an intriguing and unique website focussed on delivering a hybrid informative experience about the cities of Melbourne, Australia and Ho Chi Minh, Vietnam. The process involved ideating and research into the content as well as design ideas for the website. We then created the prototype in blender and Adobe XD.
